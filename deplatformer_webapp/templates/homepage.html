Deplatformer liberates your Facebook data

  1. Select "Facebook > Deplatform" and follow the instructions on how to export and liberate your content.
  2. Select "Facebook > Upload content" and move your data to Deplatformer.
  3. Select "Facebook > View content" to see your Facebook albums as well as your Facebook memories that you posted over the years on this day, {{ this_month }} {{ this_day }}.
  4. You can add new photos to existing Facebook albums or delete albums you no longer want to keep.
  5. You can Select "All media" to create new albums and manage this new content via Deplatformer.

Stay tuned!

This is the basic Milestone 1 release of Deplatformer. In the upcoming Milestone 2 release your uploaded content will be encrypted and three redundant copies of the encrypted data will be stored to the Filecoin network.

Subscribe to the Deplatformer newsletter to keep up to date on our progress. You can also follow us on Twitter
